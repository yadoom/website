Hello, I'm Adam Ali.


I am a student at the University of Washington Bothell, pursuing a BS in Computer Science & Software Engineering.
I expect to graduate in Spring 2019.

I grew up in the Greater Seattle Area, what many consider to be the Silicon Valley of the north, or what I like to call the Silicon Mountains. It didn't take long for me to become positively influenced by the technology culture here. With the help of friends and family, I began to poke around with all sorts of devices from early Macs to various handhelds. One day I discovered Hello, World! and I've been hooked ever since.

I am interested in computer science because I have a natural inclination to work with technology. I enjoy the challenges that it brings and solving problems that require strategy and analysis. The satisfaction I feel when overcoming a challenge and solving a complex problem is unmatched by nearly anything else. Technology itself has the power to solve problems and change lives and cultures for the better, and therefore having the ability to develop technology is much like a superpower in its own right.

I enjoy improving things and making discoveries, while learning from mistakes along the way. On the side, I have a strong interest in performance art and mountain biking.
